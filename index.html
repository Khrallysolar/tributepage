Sound Sultan

The late music maestro.
Nigerian rapper, singer and songwriter.

Personal Life

In 2009, Sound Sultan married his long-term girlfriend Chichi Morah, now known as Farida Fasasi.They had three children. On May 12, 2021, it was reported that Sound Sultan was somewhere in the USA receiving chemotherapy as he was reportedly diagnosed with Angioimmunoblastic T-cell lymphoma in the throat. He died on July 11, 2021, at the age of 44 as a result of complications from the illness. Sound Sultan was the younger brother of Nigerian musical artiste, Baba Dee.
